# Taipei River Pulse｜大台北水脈

## 中文介紹

大台北水脈（Taipei River Pulse）是一套以大台北地區五大水系為核心的互動式 WebGIS 系統，整合空間視覺化、文化觀光導覽、即時水位監測與定位服務，提供兼具教育、防災與觀光價值的數位探索平台。

本系統以淡水河、新店溪、大漢溪、基隆河及景美溪為主要研究對象，透過 Leaflet 與 OpenStreetMap 建立互動式地圖介面，使用者可自由瀏覽河流路線、歷史文化景點、河岸故事以及即時環境資訊。

系統整合經濟部水利署開放資料（Water Resources Agency Open Data API），即時取得各河川監測站水位資訊，並透過視覺化方式呈現監測站位置、水位狀態與風險區域，提升民眾對河川環境與防災知識的認識。

### 主要功能

* 五大水系空間視覺化
* 河流互動導覽與故事介紹
* 景點與文化古蹟導覽
* Google Street View 街景探索
* GPS 定位與附近景點偵測
* 徽章收集與探索紀錄
* 即時水位監測
* 水位警戒通知
* 圖層管理與空間資訊切換

### 系統架構

本系統包含三個主要子系統：

#### 1. River Spatial Visualization Subsystem

* 河流圖層顯示
* 河流路線互動
* 河流資訊查詢
* 圖層管理

#### 2. Cultural Tourism and Location-Based Guide Subsystem

* 景點導覽
* 歷史文化介紹
* Google Street View 街景導覽
* GPS 定位服務
* 徽章收集系統

#### 3. Real-Time Water-Level Monitoring Subsystem

* 水利署 Open Data API 串接
* 即時水位查詢
* 水位監測站顯示
* 水位風險熱區顯示
* 警戒通知功能

## English Introduction

Taipei River Pulse is an interactive WebGIS platform designed around the five major river systems of Greater Taipei. The system integrates spatial visualization, cultural tourism guidance, real-time water-level monitoring, and location-based services to provide an educational, disaster-awareness, and tourism-oriented exploration experience.

The platform focuses on the Tamsui River, Xindian River, Dahan River, Keelung River, and Jingmei River. Built with Leaflet and OpenStreetMap, it enables users to explore river routes, historical landmarks, cultural stories, and environmental information through an interactive map interface.

To enhance environmental awareness and flood preparedness, the system integrates the Water Resources Agency Open Data API, allowing real-time retrieval and visualization of river monitoring station data, water levels, warning thresholds, and risk zones.

### Key Features

* Visualization of the Five Major River Systems
* Interactive River Exploration
* Cultural and Heritage Tourism Guide
* Google Street View Integration
* GPS Positioning and Nearby Attraction Detection
* Badge Collection System
* Real-Time Water-Level Monitoring
* Flood Warning Notification
* GIS Layer Management

### System Architecture

The platform consists of three major subsystems:

#### 1. River Spatial Visualization Subsystem

* River Layer Visualization
* Interactive River Exploration
* River Information Display
* Layer Management

#### 2. Cultural Tourism and Location-Based Guide Subsystem

* Point of Interest (POI) Guide
* Historical and Cultural Interpretation
* Google Street View Integration
* GPS-Based Navigation
* Badge Collection Mechanism

#### 3. Real-Time Water-Level Monitoring Subsystem

* Water Resources Agency Open Data API Integration
* Real-Time Water-Level Retrieval
* Monitoring Station Visualization
* Water Risk Buffer Visualization
* Flood Warning Notification
